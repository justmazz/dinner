About Us

At Dinner and a Show, we believe in the power of community and shared experiences. We're a non-profit connecting families with a terminally ill member to generous season ticket holders and restaurants offering a free meal. No monetary donations—just heartfelt contributions to create a night to remember.

How It Works
  1. Donate Your Tickets
    Season ticket holders send their unused digital tickets to our email.
  2. Partner Restaurants Offer a Meal
    Local eateries sign up to provide a complimentary dining experience.
  3. We Make the Match
    Families in need are paired with tickets and a dinner for a special night out.

It's that simple! Together, we're making unforgettable memories in our community.
